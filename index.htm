Age Calculator

This age calculator helps you determine your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Additionally, it shows how many days, weeks, and hours have passed since your birth and provides a real-time countdown until your next birthday. This is perfect for anyone curious about their age in detail or who wants a personalized countdown for their next celebration!

How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Enter your birth date in the provided Date field.
  2. If you know your exact birth time, enter it in the Time field (optional, for more precision).
  3. Click the Calculate Age button to view your detailed age information and the next birthday countdown.
  4. If you want to reset and enter new data, click the Reset button.
  5. Switch between light and dark mode by clicking Toggle Dark Mode for a comfortable viewing experience.

About the Results

Your Age: Displays your age in detail, breaking it down into years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Days, Weeks, and Hours Since Birth: Tracks the exact amount of time that has passed since you were born. This feature is great for anyone interested in milestone tracking, such as hitting 10,000 days or 1 million hours!

Time Until Next Birthday: Provides a countdown to your next birthday, updating in real-time. It counts down in days, hours, minutes, and seconds, letting you know exactly how long until your next celebration.
